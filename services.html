What We Refer
JOBS THAT
NEED A
PERMIT.

Some jobs require a permit pull, and we don't do that work. We'll always tell you upfront, and we'll connect you with a master electrician we trust personally. You'll never leave a conversation with us without knowing exactly who to call next.

  • Panel upgrade or replacement
  • Subpanel installation
  • EV charger installation (any type)
  • New 240V dedicated circuit
  • Generator hookup / transfer switch
  • Pool or hot tub electrical
  • Service entrance / meter base work
  • Aluminum wiring remediation
  • New circuit runs through walls
  • Line-voltage exterior / landscape circuit
  • Underground conduit runs
  • Anything requiring city or county inspection
