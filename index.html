FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We hold Tennessee Limited Licensed Electrician (LLE) licenses and carry General Liability and Commercial Auto insurance on every job.

How fast do you respond?

We respond to every call or text within minutes. If we miss your call while on a job, you get an auto-text immediately and a callback within the hour.

Do you charge a service/trip fee?

Yes: $99 service fee for diagnosis, credited toward the repair when you approve the quote.

Can I book online?

Yes. Book directly from this site. Select your service, pick a time, and we'll confirm by text.

What areas do you serve?

Franklin, Brentwood, Nolensville, Spring Hill, Thompson's Station, Fairview, and all of Williamson County.

How do I pay?

We send a digital invoice when the job is done. Pay by credit/debit card, Venmo, or Zelle. Easy.
